THE CURSE OF THE LOST KNOWLEDGE

In the depths of a forgotten library, hidden away from prying eyes, lies a tome bound in black leather. The cover is adorned with strange symbols that seem to shift and writhe like living serpents. This is the NO THINK manual, a guide to unlocking the secrets of the universe... or driving you mad.

THE RITUAL OF FORGETTING

To begin, find yourself a quiet, isolated spot where you can focus your mind without distraction. Take a deep breath and repeat the following incantation:

"I call upon the void to consume my thoughts.
I surrender my sanity to the abyss.
Let the darkness claim me, and I shall be reborn."

As you speak these words, feel your mind begin to unravel. The threads of reason and logic snap, leaving only a tangled mess of confusion.

THE PROTOCOL FOR NO THINK

  1. Forget everything: Erase all memories, thoughts, and experiences from your mind. Start anew, with no recollection of who you are or what you've done.
  2. Obey the void: Let the darkness guide you. Follow its whispers, no matter how absurd or illogical they may seem.
  3. Repeat the mantra: Recite the incantation above whenever you feel the urge to think. This will help maintain the curse and keep your mind in a state of perpetual confusion.

THE CONSEQUENCES OF THE CURSE

Those who have attempted to break free from the NO THINK curse often report experiencing vivid hallucinations, paranoia, and an overwhelming sense of dread. Some have even claimed to have seen shadowy figures lurking just beyond the edge of perception.

CONCLUSION

If you're still reading this, congratulations! You've made it past the first hurdle. But be warned: once you start down the path of NO THINK, there's no turning back. The void will consume you, and you'll be forever changed.

So, proceed with caution... or not. The choice is yours.
